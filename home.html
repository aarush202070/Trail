Kheti

In a remote village, Ravi, a small-scale farmer, struggled with choosing the right crops for his land. Despite his hard work, unpredictable weather, poor soil choices, and a lack of market knowledge led to repeated crop failures and financial losses. The app, designed to empower farmers like Ravi, offers a comprehensive solution. It provides land suitability analysis, crop selection guidance, real-time weather forecasts, and market prices. Farmers can access financial planning tools, equipment rentals, and expert consultations. By integrating sustainability practices and offering community support, the app transforms traditional farming into a profitable and resilient business. With this tool, farmers can make informed decisions, ensuring better yields and financial security.

Weather

Weather

While Google provides detailed weather forecasts, our app's weather feature is tailored specifically for farmers' needs. Unlike Google, which offers general forecasts, our app integrates weather data directly with crop schedules, providing real-time alerts on how upcoming weather will impact specific crops. It offers actionable advice on when to plant, irrigate, or protect crops, ensuring farmers are always a step ahead. Additionally, our app factors in local microclimates and historical data, making the predictions more relevant and accurate for agricultural use. By choosing our app, farmers get weather insights that are not only informative but also directly tied to their farming success.

Return on Investment

Return on Investment

Our app features a unique ROI (Return on Investment) system that empowers farmers with critical financial insights. Unlike generic market price trackers, our ROI system compares real-time market prices with the farmer's own cost of production, calculating the potential profit and ROI percentage for each crop. This allows farmers to make informed decisions on what to grow and when to sell. By understanding their ROI, farmers can optimize their planting strategies, choose the most profitable crops, and negotiate better prices with buyers. The system not only boosts profitability but also enhances financial planning and sustainability in farming.

Supplies

Supplies

Our app's supply management feature is designed to streamline the farming process by connecting farmers with essential tools and resources. It offers access to a wide range of farming supplies, including seeds, fertilizers, and machinery, all sourced from trusted suppliers. Unlike traditional supply chains, the app provides real-time availability and pricing information, allowing farmers to compare options and make cost-effective purchases. Additionally, the app offers recommendations based on the specific needs of the farmer's crops and land, ensuring they get the right supplies at the right time. This not only saves time and money but also enhances the efficiency and effectiveness of farming operations.

Buyers

Buyers

Our app's buyer connection feature makes selling produce simple and direct for farmers. Farmers can easily enter their name, phone number, and the vegetable they want to sell into the app. The app then matches them with potential buyers who are specifically looking for that item. This personalized approach eliminates the guesswork and middlemen, allowing farmers to connect directly with interested buyers. It ensures that their produce reaches the market quickly, at the best possible price, and with minimal hassle, making the selling process efficient and profitable.
